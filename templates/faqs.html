Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are the admission requirements for new students?

To enroll, students must submit their birth certificate, previous school records or report card, completed application form, and two 2x2 ID photos. For transferees, a certificate of good moral character is also required.

2. When does the academic year start and end?

Flor de Grace School follows a typical academic calendar starting in August and ending in May, consisting of two semesters with midyear breaks and holidays in accordance with the Department of Education guidelines.

3. Does the school offer scholarships or financial assistance?

Yes. The school offers merit-based scholarships for top-performing students, as well as financial assistance for those in need. Applicants must fill out a scholarship form and meet the required criteria.

4. What grade levels and programs are available?

We offer Pre-school (Nursery, Kinder), Elementary (Grades 1–6), and Junior High School (Grades 7–10). We also have Senior High School programs under the Academic Track such as STEM, ABM, and HUMSS.

5. Are there extracurricular activities and clubs available?

Absolutely! We encourage holistic development through clubs such as the Science Club, Journalism Club, Dance Troupe, Student Government, Music Ensemble, and various sports activities.

6. Is online learning available?

Yes. We offer blended and full online learning options through our digital learning platform. This ensures continued education in times of disruption, with access to e-modules, virtual classes, and real-time teacher support.
